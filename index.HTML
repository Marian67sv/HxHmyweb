Nen Types

Types Description Character
Transmuter Modifies the properties of their aura to mimic external substances. The aura remains attached to the user, but its tactile or chemical nature changes (e.g., texture, temperature, or electrical current).
Conjurer Uses their aura to materialize physical, tangible objects. These objects can possess special functions or rules that go beyond their mundane counterparts, often requiring complex setup to manifest.
Specialist A category for abilities that defy the standard categorization system. It often involves high-level conceptual powers, such as manipulating time, stealing other Nen abilities, or reading memories.
Manipulator Infuses aura into external targets—animate or inanimate—to exert direct control over them. Effectiveness depends on the complexity of the medium and the "condition" required to establish control.
Emitter Focuses on detaching aura from the body and sustaining its strength at range. This allows the user to project energy blasts, create autonomous constructs, or bridge distances through space.
Enhancer Utilizes aura to amplify the innate potential of a target. By reinforcing the object's molecular structure or the user's own physiology, they achieve massive increases in raw power and durability.

Nen System

The Four Major Principles (The Fundamentals)

Ten (Enwrap / Maintain)



This is the very first step. Normally, a person's life energy leaks out and scatters constantly. Ten is the process of opening your aura nodes and keeping that energy contained, making it flow smoothly around your body like a protective shroud. What is it for? It slows down aging, keeps the body healthy, and provides a basic emotional and physical defense against standard Nen attacks.

Zetsu (Suppress / Subdue)



This is the exact opposite of Ten. Instead of containing the aura, Zetsu completely closes all your aura nodes, stopping the flow of energy to the outside world entirely. What is it for? It makes you practically invisible to the senses of other Nen users, making it the ultimate tool for stealth. It also helps relieve extreme fatigue. The Danger: Because you have absolutely no aura protecting your body, taking an attack while in Zetsu can easily be fatal.

Ren (Enhance / Intensify)



This is the ability to drastically expand and burst forward the amount of aura you produce. While Ten keeps it contained, Ren lets you overflow with raw power. What is it for? It exponentially increases your physical strength, stamina, and durability. It is the absolute foundation for any serious combat.

Hatsu (Release / Actuate)



This is the culmination of the previous three. It is the expression of your aura to carry out a specific function. In short, it is the creation of your unique special ability (like Gon’s Jajanken or Killua’s Godspeed). Your Hatsu depends entirely on your aura type.

Advanced Techniques

Gyo (Focus)



An application of Ren. It involves concentrating a large amount of aura into one specific body part. Using Gyo on your eyes allows you to see hidden aura. In (Conceal): An advanced form of Zetsu. It allows you to make your active aura completely invisible to others, even if you are casting a powerful Hatsu. It can only be countered by using Gyo on the eyes.

En (Circle)



A combination of Ten and Ren. You expand your aura into a sphere around you (ranging from meters to kilometers, depending on the user like Meruem or Pitou). Anything that enters this radius is instantly detected, down to a speck of dust. Shu (Enfold): An advanced application of Ten. It allows you to extend your aura onto an object you are holding, treating it as an extension of your own body (like Hisoka making regular playing cards as sharp as steel blades).

Ken (Fortify)



A defensive technique combining Ten and Ren. You maintain a maximum state of Ren evenly across your entire body to defend against attacks from any direction. It is incredibly effective but highly exhausting.

Ryu (Flow)



The real-time application of Ken in combat. It is the skill of shifting the percentage of aura around your body in fractions of a second (e.g., putting 70% aura in your fist to punch while leaving 30% on your body to defend).

Ko (Temper)



Taking Gyo to the absolute extreme. You use Zetsu to completely shut off aura in the rest of your body, forcing 100% of your Ren into a single point (like a fist). A hit with Ko is devastating, but it leaves the rest of your body entirely unprotected.
